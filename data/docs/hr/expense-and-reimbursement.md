# Expense & Reimbursement Policy

**Owner:** Finance · **Last updated:** 2025-04-01 · **Policy ID:** HR-EXP-003

## How to submit
Submit all expenses in **Ramp** within **30 days** of purchase with an itemized receipt.
Reimbursements are paid in the next payroll cycle after manager approval.

## Approval thresholds
- Under **$200**: manager approval only.
- **$200–$2,000**: manager + department head approval.
- Over **$2,000**: requires Finance pre-approval *before* purchase.

## Reimbursable
Travel for company business, client meals (up to $75/person), software needed for your role,
conference tickets (pre-approved), and the home office stipend (see HR-REM-002).

## Not reimbursable
Personal entertainment, commuting to a co-working space, alcohol beyond a 2-drink limit at client meals,
and any expense submitted more than 60 days after purchase.
