# Code of Conduct

**Owner:** People Team · **Last updated:** 2025-01-05 · **Policy ID:** HR-COC-001

Vela Cloud expects all employees, contractors, and contingent workers to act with integrity
and respect.

## Core expectations
Treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect. Harassment, discrimination, and
retaliation are prohibited and grounds for termination.

## Conflicts of interest
Disclose any outside employment, board seat, or financial interest in a competitor or vendor
to the People Team. Accepting gifts over **$100** from a vendor must be reported.

## Reporting concerns
Report violations to your manager, the People Team, or anonymously via the **Ethics Hotline**
(ethics@velacloud.example). All reports are investigated; retaliation against a reporter is itself a violation.
