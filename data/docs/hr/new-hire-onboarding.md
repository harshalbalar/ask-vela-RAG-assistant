# New Hire Onboarding Guide

**Owner:** People Team · **Last updated:** 2025-03-20 · **Policy ID:** HR-ONB-007

## Before day one
IT ships your laptop (see IT-LAP-014) to arrive 3 business days before your start date. Complete
your I-9 / right-to-work verification in Workday.

## Week 1
- Day 1: Welcome session, accounts setup, security training.
- Day 2: Meet your team, set up dev environment (engineering: see ENG-ARCH-008).
- Day 3–5: Shadow a teammate, complete role-specific training modules.

## 30-60-90 plan
Your manager sets a 30-60-90 day plan in your first week. A check-in happens at each milestone.

## Buddy program
Every new hire is paired with an onboarding buddy from a different team for their first month.
