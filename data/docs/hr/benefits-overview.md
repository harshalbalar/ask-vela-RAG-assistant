# Benefits Overview

**Owner:** People Team · **Last updated:** 2025-01-10 · **Policy ID:** HR-BEN-005

## Health
Vela Cloud covers **100% of employee medical, dental, and vision premiums** and 70% for dependents.
Coverage begins on your first day.

## Retirement
We match **4% of salary** into a 401(k) (or local equivalent), vesting immediately.

## Equity
Most full-time roles include stock options with a 4-year vesting schedule and a 1-year cliff.

## Learning budget
Every employee gets a **$1,500/year learning budget** for courses, books, and conferences,
claimed through the Expense process.

## Wellness
A **$60/month wellness stipend** covers gym, therapy apps, or fitness equipment.
