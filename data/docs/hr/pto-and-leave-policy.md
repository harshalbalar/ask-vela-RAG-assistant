# Paid Time Off (PTO) & Leave Policy

**Owner:** People Team · **Last updated:** 2025-03-01 · **Policy ID:** HR-PTO-004

## Annual leave
Full-time Vela Cloud employees accrue **20 days** of paid vacation per year (1.67 days/month).
Unused vacation rolls over up to a maximum of **5 days** into the next calendar year. Any
balance above 5 days on December 31 is forfeited.

## Sick leave
Employees receive **10 paid sick days** per year, separate from vacation. Sick days do not
roll over. A doctor's note is required for any sick absence longer than 3 consecutive days.

## Public holidays
Vela Cloud observes 11 public holidays based on the employee's country of residence. Remote
employees follow their local national holiday calendar.

## Requesting time off
Submit requests in **Workday** at least **5 business days** in advance for vacation of 3+ days.
Your manager approves or declines within 2 business days. Sick leave does not require advance approval.

## Unpaid leave
Unpaid leave of absence may be granted at manager and People Team discretion for up to 90 days.
