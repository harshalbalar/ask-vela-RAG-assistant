# Parental Leave Policy

**Owner:** People Team · **Last updated:** 2025-02-10 · **Policy ID:** HR-PAR-006

## Eligibility
All full-time employees who have completed **6 months** of service are eligible.

## Birthing parent
The birthing parent receives **16 weeks** of fully paid leave, which may begin up to 2 weeks
before the due date.

## Non-birthing / adoptive parent
The non-birthing or adoptive parent receives **10 weeks** of fully paid leave, to be taken within
12 months of the birth or placement.

## Phased return
Returning parents may work a reduced 80% schedule at full pay for their first 4 weeks back.

## Notice
Notify the People Team at least **8 weeks** before your intended leave start date where possible.
