# Remote Work Policy

**Owner:** People Team · **Last updated:** 2025-01-15 · **Policy ID:** HR-REM-002

Vela Cloud is a **remote-first** company. Employees may work from anywhere within their
country of employment without prior approval.

## Working hours
Core collaboration hours are **10:00–15:00 in your team's primary timezone**. Outside core
hours, employees manage their own schedule. All-hands meetings are recorded for those who cannot attend.

## Home office stipend
New employees receive a **one-time $750 home office stipend** for desk, chair, and peripherals,
claimed via the Expense process (see HR-EXP-003). An additional **$50/month** internet allowance
is paid automatically.

## Working abroad
Employees may work from a different country for up to **30 days per year** for travel, but must
notify the People Team in advance for tax and compliance reasons. Stays beyond 30 days require approval.

## Co-working
Vela Cloud reimburses co-working memberships up to **$200/month** with manager approval.
