# Laptop Provisioning & Equipment

**Owner:** IT · **Last updated:** 2025-02-18 · **Doc ID:** IT-LAP-014

## Standard hardware
- Engineering: MacBook Pro 14" (M-series, 32GB RAM).
- Non-engineering: MacBook Air 13" (16GB RAM).
- Windows or Linux laptops available on request for specific roles.

## Setup
Laptops ship pre-enrolled in **Jamf** (MDM) with disk encryption (FileVault) and MFA. First boot
walks you through Okta sign-in. Do not disable MDM or disk encryption.

## Refresh & returns
Laptops are refreshed every **3 years**. On departure, return all equipment within 5 business days
using the prepaid shipping label IT provides.

## Repairs
For hardware issues, open a ticket in the IT portal; loaner devices are available for urgent cases.
