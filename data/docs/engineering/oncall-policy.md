# On-Call Policy

**Owner:** SRE · **Last updated:** 2025-04-20 · **Doc ID:** ENG-ONC-010

## Rotation
Each engineering team runs a weekly on-call rotation in PagerDuty. A shift runs Monday 10:00
to the following Monday 10:00. Engineers should not be on-call more than 1 week in 4.

## Expectations
Acknowledge pages within **5 minutes** during a shift. Keep a laptop and stable internet reachable.
You are not expected to do project work while actively firefighting.

## Compensation
On-call engineers receive a **$500 stipend per week-long shift**, plus time-off-in-lieu for any
night (22:00–07:00) page that exceeds 1 hour of active work.

## Escalation
If the primary on-call does not acknowledge within 5 minutes, PagerDuty escalates to the secondary,
then to the engineering manager.
