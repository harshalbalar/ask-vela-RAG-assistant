# Support Escalation Process

**Owner:** Customer Support · **Last updated:** 2025-05-06 · **Doc ID:** OPS-ESC-019

## Tiers
- **Tier 1** — first response, common issues, account questions. First response SLA: 4 business hours.
- **Tier 2** — technical troubleshooting, config issues. Escalated when Tier 1 cannot resolve in 1 day.
- **Tier 3 / Engineering** — suspected bugs or platform issues. Routed via a Jira ticket to the owning team.

## Priority
P1 (production down for customer) gets a 1-hour first response and may trigger an engineering incident
(see ENG-INC-009). P2/P3 follow standard SLAs.

## Handoffs
When escalating, include reproduction steps, tenant_id, timestamps, and any error codes. Never share
a customer's Confidential data in a public channel (see IT-SEC-013).
